The download button on google drive requires third party cookies.

    W   H   Y

BUT you can download using the direct url. Typical file sharing url:

    https://drive.google.com/file/d/<FILE_ID>/view?usp=sharing

Equivalent download url:

    https://drive.google.com/uc?export=download&id=<FILE_ID>

More information and other URL tricks for google drive:
https://www.labnol.org/internet/direct-links-for-google-drive/28356/
